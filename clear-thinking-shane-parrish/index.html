Book Review: Clear Thinking by Shane Parrish

10 Aug 2024
  1. review
  2. book
  3. reading

Clear Thinking is a book written by Shane Parrish(FS Blog fame). This book talks about the decision making framework and how to use ordinary moments to make big impact.

Things I liked

  1. I like the detailed overview of decision making framework and how to use it to make real change
  2. How mentors/examplars are helpful
  3. reversible vs non reversible decisions

I am going to utilize some of the frameworks mentioned in the book to make better decisions/choices in my life.

rise of the robots by martin ford

December 25, 2016
general blog review

I have been reading Rise of the robots from some time and finally finished it today. It was a real good read and talk about impact of automation and jobs and society in general. This topics talked about in the book can not be more timely. Automation is coming and turning out to be a job killer. So the book talk about some of the actions that can be taken to help society in general. The book also talks about Artificial Intelligence, nano-technology etc.

Overall a good book and I will recommend it for sure.
